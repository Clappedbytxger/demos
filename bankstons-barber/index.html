A Barnesville Tradition

Almost ninety years. One family. One town.

Bankston's has been the place Barnesville comes to for a haircut — and a conversation — for generations.

1938

The original shop

Billy Sr. opens Bankston Barber Shop on Main Street. From day one it's more than haircuts — it's where the men of Barnesville come to socialize.

1975

The Hair Hut

Billy Jr. opens the Hair Hut, a progressive hair salon offering women's and men's haircuts — bringing modern styling to town.

2007

Two shops become one

Dan Bankston merges the barber shop and the salon into today's Bankston's Barber Shop and Salon — continuing the family legacy.

The Bankston family carries 130+ years of combined barbering experience — and it shows in every cut.
